Frequently Asked Questions

Where are you located?

Dias Designs is strictly an onlined-based business.

How long do I have to wait to receive a customised product?

A custom-made item usually takes 3-5 days to be completed. However, it may take a few more days depending on the design, colour and size of the item.

Do you take measurements?

No, most syles require a bra size(for eg. 32B) and a hip measurement/accurate pants size.

Do you do deliveries?

Yes we do. We deliver to Spanish Town, Half-way-Tree and Portmore

What is the cost for delivery?

The cost for delivery depends on the location, however; the price ranges from $500-$1000.
